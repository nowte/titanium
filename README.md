## Titanium Projects

**Titanium Projects** is a platform designed for anyone who wants to contribute to the open-source world.  
We provide a variety of **CLI tools** and invite you to collaborate on developing these tools, adding new features, and fixing bugs.  

Our goal isn’t just writing code; it’s about learning, sharing experiences, and growing the open-source community.  
We welcome developers of all levels: whether you’re a beginner or an experienced programmer, your contributions are highly valued.  

Join our community and become part of the journey by connecting on our **[Discord server][discord]**.  
There you can exchange ideas, ask questions, and actively contribute to our projects.

## Contributing

Please read our [contribution guidelines][contributing] before submitting a pull request.  
We welcome contributions of all kinds, not just code!  

- If you’re looking for ideas, check out issues labeled **[good first issue][good-first-issue]**.  
- If you have questions about the project, ask them on **[Discord][discord]**.  
- Before creating a new issue or pull request, always check if a similar one already exists.  
- Don’t rush your contributions—take your time and ensure everything is done correctly.
